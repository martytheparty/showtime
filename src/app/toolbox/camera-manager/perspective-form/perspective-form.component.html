Field Of View (FOV) The range of the observable world that is visible. Aspect Ratio The ratio of an image's width to its height. Near The nearest part of the scene that will be rendered. Far The farthest part of the scene that will be rendered. x position The position of the camera on the x axis. y position The position of the camera on the y axis. z position The position of the camera on the z axis. x look-at The position on the x axis that the camera is pointed at. y look-at The position on the y axis that the camera is pointed at. z look-at The position on the z axis that the camera is pointed at.
